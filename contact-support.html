Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to emails within 24 hours, live chat within 2-3 minutes, and phone calls are answered immediately during business hours.

What information should I include in my support request?

Please include your account email, course name (if applicable), detailed description of the issue, and any error messages you're seeing.

Can I get help with course content?

Yes! Our support team can help with technical issues, but for course-specific questions, we recommend reaching out to the course instructor or using the course discussion forum.

What if I need urgent help?

For urgent issues, please use our live chat or call us directly. We prioritize urgent requests and will respond as quickly as possible.

Do you offer support in multiple languages?

Currently, we offer support in English, Spanish, and French. We're working on adding more languages to better serve our global community.

How can I track my support ticket?

Once you submit a support request, you'll receive a confirmation email with a ticket number. You can use this number to track the status of your request.
